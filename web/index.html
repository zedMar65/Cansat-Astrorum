About

Astrorum is a CanSat team competing in the Lithuanian CanSat competition with a focus on hazard assessment and environmental monitoring. Our goal is to design and build a small-scale satellite capable of evaluating environmental risks by measuring air quality, dust particles, radiation levels, and other key factors. The satellite will collect data to help assess hazards and support monitoring systems, offering insights for improving safety and environmental awareness. With a focus on teamwork, creativity, and precision, Astrorum blends engineering, programming, and design to create a reliable and innovative solution. We are driven by a passion for science, problem-solving, and pushing the limits of what small satellites can achieve in practical applications.
